### Step 2: Sign Up

1. You'll be asked to choose an email address
2. **Option A**: Use subdomain (easiest)

   - This is FREE and works immediately
3. **Option B**: Use your own domain (if you have one)
   - Example: `admin@mombasashishabongs.com`
   - Requires domain verification (takes 10 minutes)

### Step 4: Verify Email
1. Check your personal email
2. Click verification link
3. Done!

### Step 5: Access Your Email
1. Go to: https://mail.zoho.com/
2. Login with your new email
3. You now have a professional business email!
